About

infoTech Niagara's yearly shadow program creates the opportunity for students to experience a day in the life of a worker in a field they want to explore.

  • Step 1:

    Tell Us Who You Are.

    If you're a student, let us know what you're interested in experiencing. If you're a company, let us know what you do!

  • Step 2:

    We Play Matchmaker.

    We go through all of the applications and match Students and Companies based on their interests and offerings.

  • Step 3:

    Everyone Learns!

    Students get to check out possible career paths. Employers get to learn from students about new technologies and current trends in academia.
